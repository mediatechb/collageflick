Support

Need help with templates, camera, or saving images? Try these quick steps first.

Common fixes

  • Restart the app and ensure you have the latest version.
  • Check camera & photo permissions in Settings → Privacy.
  • Free up storage space before exporting high‑resolution images.

Feature tips

  • Movie Photo Maker: pick a frame template, then add movie stills or your own photos.
  • Selfie with Movie: use the front camera, align your face, then swap backgrounds/posters.
  • Edit tools: crop, rotate, filters, and caption boxes for reviews or rankings.

Report a problem

Send steps to reproduce, device model, OS version, and screenshots to our support email below.

FAQ

Can I use my own photos with templates?

Yes. You can mix your device photos, selfies, and movie stills/posters in any collage or frame template.


Where do movie images come from?

Movie information and images may be sourced from public APIs or licensed databases (e.g., TMDb). This app is not affiliated with or endorsed by any studio. Always review the image licensing and your local laws before commercial use.


Why can’t I save in full resolution?

Large exports require sufficient storage and memory. Close other apps and ensure you have space available, then try again.
